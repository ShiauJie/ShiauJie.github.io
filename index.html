About Me

Currently, my research is about type Ia supernova. I study type Ia supernovae from the hydrodynamical aspect. We focus on how the companion star will evolve after the explosion. I am curious about how analytical and numerical solution complements each other. I had been an intern student at both physics (ASIOP) and astronomy (ASIAA) institute in Academia Sinica. I had explored various research fields, especially astronomy and high energy physics.
I also enjoy teaching. Now I am teaching students in the challenge at NTHU resource center.
